Working of Healthy Heart

•72 beats per minute is a good heart rate

•A normal resting heart rate ranges from 60 to 100 beats per minute

•The mean heart rate during the febrile period was 84.0 beats per minute

•When the temperature rose by 1 degree C, the heart rate increases on the average by 8.5 beats per minute
